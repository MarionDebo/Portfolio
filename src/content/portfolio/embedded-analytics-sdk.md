## Context

Our customers wanted analytics inside their own product — not a link to a third-party dashboard. They needed white-label charts, filters, and drill-downs that felt native to their UX.

## The problem

Our existing export approach (iframes + shared links) was fragile, couldn't be styled, and created security headaches for enterprise IT. We were losing deals to competitors who offered an SDK.

## What I did

I ran a build vs. buy analysis and made the case for building. Over 12 weeks I defined the SDK's API surface — component contracts, auth model, query caching strategy, and a theming system that covered 90% of enterprise customisation needs without code.

I worked directly with the engineering lead to scope the MVP, prioritise capabilities by deal-blocking frequency, and define the integration guide that became the primary sales artefact.

## Impact

- **40+ enterprise clients** adopted the SDK in the first two quarters
- **DAU/MAU ratio increased ×2.4** versus the iframe approach
- Won 3 competitive deals directly attributed to SDK availability
- Sub-200ms p95 query latency SLA met across all clients
